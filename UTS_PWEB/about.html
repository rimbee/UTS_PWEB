Our Story

Founded in Milan in 1913 by Mario Prada as a leather goods shop, Prada has become one of the world's most prestigious luxury brands, synonymous with cutting-edge fashion, uncompromising quality, and avant-garde design.

Under the leadership of Miuccia Prada, the brand has consistently challenged conventions and redefined modern luxury, blending tradition with innovation.

Our Mission

"What you wear is how you present yourself to the world, especially today, when human contacts are so quick. Fashion is instant language." Miuccia Prada

Prada Timeline

1913

Founding of Prada

Mario Prada and his brother Martino establish Fratelli Prada in Milan, specializing in luxury leather goods.

1978

Miuccia Prada Takes Over

Miuccia Prada, Mario's granddaughter, joins the company and begins transforming it into a fashion powerhouse.

1985

The Iconic Nylon Backpack

Prada launches the black nylon backpack, revolutionizing luxury fashion with its minimalist aesthetic.

1993

Prada Foundation Established

Miuccia Prada and Patrizio Bertelli establish the Fondazione Prada to promote contemporary art and culture.

Sustainability

Prada is committed to responsible luxury, with initiatives like Re-Nylon - a line made from regenerated nylon that can be recycled indefinitely without quality loss.

Our sustainability strategy focuses on circular economy principles, climate action, and social responsibility throughout our supply chain.
